About

Innovations for a Better Tomorrow (IBeTo) is a technical innovations competition hosted by Govt. Model Engineering College, Kochi as part of the annual national level technical symposium, Excel 2020. IBeTo is a nationwide initiative that provides a platform that allows young innovative minds to demonstrate how their ideas can help make a better tomorrow.

Innovations for a Better Tomorrow (IBeTo) is the flagship event of EXCEL. It is a national level pursuit for socially relevant and technologically innovative ideas with the potential to trigger the next big social change. IBeTo gives the right platform and mentorship to launch the most technically innovative and revolutionary ideas and to develop them into a final product.

Rewards

  • The best projects will be awarded cash prizes worth INR 1,00,000/-
  • Top 3 teams will get a direct entry into the final round of the Idea Fest competition by KSUM.
  • Selected teams will get a chance to receive incubation & office space by InQ.
  • Certificate of Participation will be awarded, signed by the concerned authorities.
  • As per KTU regulations, socially relevant projects will be given 50 activity points.
